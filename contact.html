Frequently Asked Questions

How do I become a member?

You can join Concordia by contacting our membership team. Fill out the membership form and attend an orientation session. Contact us at membership@concordia.com for more details.

What are the membership benefits?

Members enjoy access to all club events, networking opportunities, exclusive workshops, discounts on facilities, and priority booking for special events.

How often are events held?

We organize 50+ events throughout the year, including monthly networking sessions, quarterly conferences, and special annual celebrations.

Can I bring guests to events?

Most events allow members to bring guests. Special permission may be required for certain exclusive events. Please check event details or contact us for clarification.

What is your cancellation policy?

Cancellations must be made at least 48 hours in advance for refunds. For specific event policies, please refer to the event details or contact our events team.

How can I sponsor the club?

We offer various sponsorship packages at Platinum, Gold, and Silver levels. Contact our sponsorship coordinator at sponsors@concordia.com for more information.
