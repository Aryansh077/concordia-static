ABOUT CONCORDIA

Who We Are

Concordia club is dedicated to promoting international awareness, multiculturalism, and creating global opportunities. The club focuses on connections with international organizations to host prestigious events like Model United Nations (MUN) and TEDx, etc. We also aim to platform organising international festivals on and off campus including activities such as international food and dance, networking events with international students and debates on global topics. All for an audience of young adults and university students in the city, to open up new avenues for their careers. Our initiatives also include hosting and facilitating career & study abroad opportunities, and organizing global innovation challenges, film nights, and language exchanges. The word Concordia refers to the ancient Roman goddess of harmony or peace, two of the values we aim to promote in this club.
